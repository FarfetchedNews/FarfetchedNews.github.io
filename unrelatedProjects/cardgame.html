Low Card

Directions
You and your opponent both flip your own cards marked "flip card". The objective of the game is to have the lowest sum of all of the cards. You may choose 3 cards marked "draw". If you flip a 1-4 from the draw pile, you may reroll one of your cards by clicking on the number. If you flip a 6-8 from the draw pile, you may reroll one of your opponents cards. Numbers 5 and 9 are trash and do nothing.
